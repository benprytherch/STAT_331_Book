7.3.1 Random effects

â€¢ Every predictor variable weâ€™ve seen this semester has been one for which we estimate and interpret a slope coefficient.

â€¢ â€œRandomâ€ effects are treated as having â€œrandomâ€ coefficients drawn from some distribution. We wonâ€™t estimate individual coefficients, but we will estimate the variance of the distribution from which they came.

â€¢ Our main motivation right now is to have a method of accounting for repeated measurements on the same subjects.
